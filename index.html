Personal Values

Rene Magritte's "Personal Values" painting is recreated here in 3D. Not only was the environment modeled and textured, but the scene could be explored in virtual reality.

Character Book Series

The following three books represent an exploration of the interaction between the narrative character of the book and the reader. The first book has the character interact with the actual pages of the book, while the second talks directly to the reader, and the last is almost a type of game for the reader to play.

Tessalacards

These cards are an experiment in tessalation, maximalism, and personification. As seen on the back of the cards, the characters tessalate forever, and the hexagonal shape of the cards allows them to be placed side by side.
